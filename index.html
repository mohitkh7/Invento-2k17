Competitions

IET DAVV Indore presents one of the biggest tech fest of central India "INVENTO 2k17". The fest would be a 2 day event that would run all throughout the day from 17-18 september 2017. INVENTO brings with it a lot of competitions and workshops as well,precisely, we have workshops on IC engines, 3d printing , robotics ,ethical hacking ,construction project management and much more. Engineering students from all branch and passouts can get benefit from the workshops. Apart from these workshop ,we have workshops on IOT , cloud computing ,android app development. We also have Inter college competitions which are our major prize winning event for you. This includes competitions on roborace, coding , hacking ,circuitry and much more. To add to the our popular events list, we have motivational session by many distinctive personalities as well.
